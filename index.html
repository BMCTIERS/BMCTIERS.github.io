Rules

Keep the competition fun, fair, and safe for everyone.

  • No cheating.
  • No harassment or toxic behaviour.
  • Rage‑baiting must stay fun and non‑harmful.
  • Proof is required for all submissions (screenshots or clips).
  • One account per player.
  • Staff / mods have final say on ranks and tiers.
